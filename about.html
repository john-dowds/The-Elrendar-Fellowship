Campaigns

We run both long-form narrative arcs and short “one-shot” adventures. Long arcs advance seasonally with clear starts and ends, expectations, and brief recaps along the way. One-shots are opt-in, low-prep nights that welcome new members.

  • Season Structure: 6–10 weeks per arc with mid-season interludes.
  • Session Cadence: 1–2 events per week; timezone-friendly scheduling.
  • Recaps & Logs: Synopsis posts, artifact details, and dialogue summaries adopted into the guild history tab after each session.
  • On-Ramps: Open call side-quests to introduce characters organically.

Interested in DMing? We provide mentorship, encounter kits, and a shared NPC/Location bank to keep tone and continuity consistent.

Systems

We use the Elrendar Sword & Shields (ESS) Roleplay Combat System for gameplay, an original system that we adjust as needed to ensure the right fit for the story. Our default rules emphasize narrative focus, fast resolution, strong character expression, and minimal out-of-character friction.

  • Primary: Roll-then-emote RP with broadly permissive outcomes.
  • Dice Systems: Simple d20 checks for all outcomes.
  • Safety Tools: Combat assists & interventions, narrative pauses, and fully respected content boundaries.
  • Canon Fit: Aligned with existing lore and community norms.

All campaign histories are saved and preserved both here on the website, and will also appear in the Elven Historical Chronicle.

PvE Content

We bridge immersive RP with end-game play. Join for story-driven dungeon, delve, and raid RP; OOC high end raiding; and mythic key nights with an inclusive, teach-friendly culture.

  • Story Runs: In-character objectives layered onto normal/heroic runs.
  • Keys & Raids: Weekly schedule with flexible role coverage and guidance.
  • Onboarding: Mentoring and practice nights where possible.
  • Accessibility: Clear comms, flexible pacing, improvement focused dialogue.

Whether you’re here for narrative immersion, progression, or both—we’ve got a lane that respects your time and playstyle.

Magical Instruction

Organized by Aelendan Shatterstar, our Magical Instruction courses run multiple sessions each month. Attendees can expect practical experimentation with both new and old forms of magic, lectures, deeper research and study, dueling, and more.

  • Structure: Rotating sessions with varied instructors and focuses—practical, theoretical, and competitive.
  • Accessibility: Open to both guild members and the general public.
  • Leadership: Anyone may volunteer to lead sessions with prior approval.
  • Engagement: Blends experimentation, lore study, and in-character sparring.

These courses foster a shared space for magical growth and collaboration. Whether you’re a student of the arcane or simply curious, the doors remain open.
