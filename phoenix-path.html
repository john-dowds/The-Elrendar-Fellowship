Quick Rules

    Set Up & Movement:

  • 12×12 board; three lanes (Left flank, Phoenix Path, Right Flank); two back rows per side (Guard, and Ban'dinoriel). Each player places their sanctum piece at the start.
  • Any piece reaching the enemy back row can reverse direction on future moves at will.
  • Each player has 18 pieces (6 of each type). One piece moves per turn; leap to capture; multi-leap allowed. Prongs indicate movement direction.

    • Ways to Win:

  • Ascension: Place 3-4 pieces in the enemy back row (1 in the sanctum, 1 along the Phoenix Path, 2 anywhere else.) Sanctums placed inside the Phoenix Row count as two.
  • Total Collapse: Reduce your opponent to only two pieces, and you'll win by Total Collapse.
  • Check your scores in the W (Win) tab. Browser cache reset may erase stats.
